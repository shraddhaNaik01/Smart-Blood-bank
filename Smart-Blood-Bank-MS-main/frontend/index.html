Saving Lives Through Smart Management

Our system digitally connects donors, stock, and recipients in real-time. Register as a donor and contribute to a smarter, faster emergency response system.

Core Functionalities

Donor Portal

Register, check eligibility, and view donation history. Your eligibility is automatically validated based on age and time since the last donation.

Recipient Portal

Request blood, view current stock availability across hospitals, and track the real-time status and compatibility of your request.

Hospital Dashboard

Manage blood stock, validate expiry dates, approve/reject requests, and update donation records linked to inventory.

About Our System

Our platform is built to simplify and digitalize the entire blood supply chain. We use a centralized database system (Node.js/MySQL) to ensure transparency, real-time stock checks, and robust compatibility validation to save critical time during emergencies.
